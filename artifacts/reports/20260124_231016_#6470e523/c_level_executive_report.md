## Problem & Context

- **Business Challenge:** The company previously lacked a single, reliable source of truth for customer, product, and sales data, resulting in delayed reporting, inconsistent metrics, and missed opportunities for targeted action.
- **Stakeholders Impacted:** Sales, marketing, product management, and executive leadership all depend on timely, accurate data to drive revenue growth and operational efficiency.
- **Success Criteria:** Success is defined by the delivery of accurate, audit-ready business metrics, improved visibility into key performance drivers, and the ability to act on insights with confidence.

---

## Solution Overview

- **What the Solution Does:** The solution brings together all relevant customer, product, and sales information into a single, business-ready view. Executive dashboards and key performance indicators are now available for immediate decision-making.
- **Value Creation:** This enables leadership to identify high-value customer segments, monitor product performance, and respond to market trends in real time. The solution also supports compliance and audit requirements by ensuring data integrity and traceability.
