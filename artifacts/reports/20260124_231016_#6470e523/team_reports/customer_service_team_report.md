## Risks & Dependencies

- **Data Pipeline Reliability:** Any disruption in real-time data flow could delay issue detection and response.
- **Cross-Department Coordination:** Dependencies on IT for dashboard integration and on Sales/Marketing for accurate customer segmentation.
- **Change Management:** Staff adaptation to new workflows and tools is critical; requires ongoing training and support.
- **Seasonality Forecast Accuracy:** Inaccurate seasonality detection could lead to under- or over-staffing.

**Mitigation:** Establish regular cross-team syncs, monitor pipeline health, and maintain agile training programs.
